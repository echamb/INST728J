About Me

Hi! My name is Emily. I am an artist, designer, and educator in Baltimore, MD. I work at the Baltimore Museum of Art in the Education Department running public programs and in-gallery educational experiences. My goal is to create tools that help visitors to better understand artworks, concepts, and histories in informal educational spaces, such as museums.

I grew up in Baltimore, MD in a neighborhood called Bolton Hill. We lived there until I was 15 years old and then my family moved to a neighborhood called Roland Park. After graduating high school, I went to college in Chicago, Illinois, which focused on fine arts. I lived in Chicago for seven years going to school and working at a number of different museums and companies. In 2015, I moved back to Baltimore to be closer to my family. I now live with my boyfriend in a neighborhood in the city called Patterson Park.
